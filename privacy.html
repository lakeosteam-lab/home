Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 21, 2026

LakeOS is designed to be simple, fast, and respectful of user privacy. We collect no personal data.

1. Data Collection

LakeOS does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data. No accounts, no tracking, no telemetry, no device identifiers.

2. Local System Data

Some local configuration files may be created to save your settings, but these stay on your device.

3. Third-Party Components

LakeOS uses components from other open-source projects. Their privacy policies may apply:

  • Ubuntu
  • KDE

4. Internet Connectivity

LakeOS does not contact external servers by default. Network activity only occurs when installing software, updating packages, or accessing online services.

5. Children’s Privacy

LakeOS does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including minors.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as LakeOS evolves. Continued use means acceptance of any changes.
